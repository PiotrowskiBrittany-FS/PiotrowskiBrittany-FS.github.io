Why did we make this?

Time management is important for everyone and is extremely difficult for people like me, who have ADHD and tend to forget or simply lose track of projects or lose interest. Personally, I have found that using my own tools helps with managing my time, and I recently started following a YouTube channel by Jessica McCabe that helps me out and helps me to figure out a schedule system that works, which is making a set time to work with zero distractions for the 25 minute time and getting a 5 minute reward. I legitimately have 50 alarms all set for tasks that I need to do and turning those off and on constantly is frustrating. This is why I want to design a product that helps with these types of issues. In Jessica’s TedTalk she explains how difficult some of these issues are as someone with ADHD. Having an actual product created for like minds is something that I feel is necessary to help with remembering appointments, doing homework assignments on time or early, getting work done on time. This product would take out the frustrations of organization out of time management. Those who use Strive Simple have shown they are more productive than when they use other apps and websites that are meant for time management

How Can This Help?

  • Better Organization
  • Better Time Management Skills
  • More Personal Time
  • No More Procrastination
  • No More Being late to appointments and meetings
  • Help to ensure there is plenty of time for each activity planned
